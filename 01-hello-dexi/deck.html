07 / 12
Listen vs. Reach Out

One word changes for real hardware.

udpin

We listen. The drone talks to us. Used in Lessons 1–9 (simulator).

udpout

We reach out. We talk to the drone. Used in Lesson 10 (real DEXI).

Same Python code. Only the address changes when you fly real hardware.

08 / 12
Why 14540?

PX4 reserves two ports.

14540

SDK / offboard control. This is us.

14550

Ground stations like QGroundControl.

PX4 happily talks to both at once.
